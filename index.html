Our Services

CPE Daycare

CPE, or early childhood centres, are non-profit private corporations, managed by a board of parents, entirely subsidized by the Government. They provide daycare services in a dedicated space, usually to 80 children organized in age groups, with a fully trained educator in each group, all year round. They follow a learn-through-play educational program and are very close to European preschool or kindergarten programs in terms of organization. Parents are required to contribute only $7 a day ($140 a month), regardless of income.

Subsidized family daycare providers take care of up to 6 children at their place. Although they are self-employed, the quality of their services (home safety, training, educational program) is controlled by Coordinating Offices (“BC” in French). Parents’ contribution is $7 a day, as in.

RSG Home Daycare

Ready, Set, Grow! provides outstanding child care services for the multi-ethnic community of Flushing, Queens. Our establishment began as a family day care center in April of 2003. Through hard work, determination, and our devotion towards children and learning, we expanded into a full-time day care center in less than a year. Due to our success in education and the support from our community, we moved into a new and more spacious location in March 2011 for our children to thrive. Here, our children are enjoying a much wider range of experience in their learning.

Ready, Set, Grow! offers Nursery, Toddler, and Prekindergarten services for our community. Our curriculum has always provided children with experiences that stimulate their learning in all developmental areas---- social, emotional, physical, and intellectual. While there are high expectations for all children, we do not expect that all the children perform the same tasks and achieve the same skills as their peers.

About US

Welcome to Day Care!


We are a non-profit centre, licensed by the Ministry of Education. We provide care for up to 24 children. Operating year round, the daycare is located in Hyderabad, within the grounds of the historic Friends House. The daycare has been operating at this location for over forty years. Friends Day Care offers full-day learning for children aged 5 weeks to 6 years, with a private, in-house Kindergarten program available.
At this daycare, our goal is to produce happy, intelligent, well-adjusted children. Here you will find a nurturing, educational environment for children of ages 3 months to 6 years. We recognize that each child has unique talents, learning styles and personalities. We are committed to working within each child’s comfort zone to ensure optimal social, emotional and educational results.
